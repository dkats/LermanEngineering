GENTL Mask

Due to the client confidentiality, I am unable to share my all of my experience online.

While the supply chain works to backfill the supply shortage of protective masks, EPAM Continuum created a simple, ease to manufacture design that can be quickly and locally manufactured to fill the supply gap due to COVID-19. See the video here. The GENTL Mask is an open source design that utilizes readily available materials and incredibly simple manufacturing processes to enable localized manufacturing.

100,000+ masks have been produced and donated to healthcare workers. This is still an on-going project with more masks still being produced.
