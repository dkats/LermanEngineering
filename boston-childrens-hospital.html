The Pediatric Simulator Program at Boston Children’s Hospital is the first hospital-based simulator at a teaching hospital in New England. SIMPeds SIM Engineering division ‘personalizes pediatrics’ by seamlessly integrating high fidelity simulator and device manufacturing into the SIMPeds curriculum development pathway for training, surgical planning and care delivery.

Mold Design

At SIMPeds I worked with a lot of mold design for silicone and urethane. Most molds were 3D printed on an EOS Formiga P 110.

Micro Linear Actuators

These are fully custom linear actuators used to move a mechanical head. They are powered by a micro servo motor and all other components were 3D printed.
